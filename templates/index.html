Game Rules

How to play Othello

  • 01The board is 8 × 8. Four discs are placed in the centre at the start.
  • 02Black always moves first.
  • 03A move is valid only if it traps at least one opponent disc between your new disc and another of your discs.
  • 04All trapped opponent discs are flipped to your colour.
  • 05If you have no valid move, your turn is passed to your opponent.
  • 06The game ends when the board is full or neither player can move.
  • 07The player with the most discs wins.
  • 08Green dots indicate squares where you can legally play.
